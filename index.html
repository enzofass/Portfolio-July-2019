Meet
Enzo Fassioli

Former Electrical Controls Engineer with a newly discovered passion for web and software development. Coming on the last stretch of a 12 week boot-camp I feel like I have work proficient knowledge in following technologies: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JQuery, Bootstrap, Node.js, relational, non-relational databases and passionate about continuing to explore this fascinating world of web development.
